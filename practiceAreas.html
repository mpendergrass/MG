Practice Areas

Our firm handles legal matters in the following practice areas:

  • Insurance Coverage and Bad Faith
  • Premises Liability
  • Insurance Defense
  • Trucking Defense
  • Products Liability
  • Construction Defects
  • Medical and Professional Malpractice Defense
  • Workers' Compensation

Insurance Coverage & Bad Faith

The Firm conducts a full array of insurance coverage litigation and counseling including both casualty and property claims, including automobile, homeowners, commercial general liability, excess/umbrella, employment practices and other insurance policies. The Firm also defends against insurers for bad faith, and conducts investigations and defends cases involving arson, fraud, material misrepresentation and other insured misconduct.

Premises Liability

The Firm has a large practice defending retail and other businesses in cases involving slip/trip/fall, drownings, and other dangerous conditions, negligent security and certain personal injury claims including defamation, false arrest, sexual molestation and other miscellaneous claims against the owners of real estate and the businesses they operate.

Insurance Defense

This includes the defense of insureds for many personal lines and commercial lines carriers for bodily injury and personal injury claims resulting from automobile accidents and miscellaneous other events not included within the firm’s Premises Liability, Trucking Defense or Products Liability practice areas.

Trucking Defense

The Firm devotes a substantial portion of its practice to defending transportation industry members in bodily injury and property damage claims arising out of their trucking operations.

Products Liability

The Firm defends manufacturers, suppliers and retailers in cases involving allegedly dangerous or defective products.

Construction Defects

The Firm defends general contractors, developers, product manufacturers, subcontractors and design professionals in commercial and residential defect cases. The Firm is also actively involved in insurance coverage disputes on behalf of insurers in this area.

Medical & Professional Malpractice Defense

The Firm defends medical, dental, optometric and pharmacy malpractice cases along with the defense of accounting malpractice.

Workers’ Compensation

The Firm defends both insured and self-insured employers defending against Workers’ Compensation claims. For employers located or conducting business in the state of South Carolina, employers with employees traveling through or injured in South Carolina, and South Carolina-based employers conducting business anywhere in the country, we promptly provide you with a thorough and honest analysis of settlement and litigation options.
